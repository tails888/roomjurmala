Frequently asked

Answers about venue rental

The essentials on event types, hourly rental, guest count, equipment and booking.

What events is ROOM Jūrmala suitable for?

The venue is suitable for children’s birthdays, family celebrations, creative workshops, photoshoots, yoga and fitness classes, seminars, trainer-led sessions and children’s camps in Kauguri, Jūrmala.

Can the venue be rented by the hour?

Yes, ROOM Jūrmala is available for hourly rental. It is useful for classes, photoshoots, workshops, seminars and shorter events when you need the venue for a specific time.

Can the space be adapted for different events?

Yes, the venue can be arranged for a party, class, workshop, photoshoot or small seminar. Tables, chairs, a small kitchen and an open activity area are available.

Is the venue suitable for children’s birthdays?

Yes, the venue has a play area, soft play elements, room for activities and a place for a celebration table. It works well for children’s birthdays and small family celebrations in Jūrmala.

Can the venue be used for family celebrations and private events?

Yes, ROOM Jūrmala can be used for family celebrations, small gatherings and private events. It is not a formal hall, so it works well for a warm and cozy atmosphere.

Can workshops and creative classes be held there?

Yes, the venue is suitable for painting, crafts, cooking, drawing and other creative workshops. There is a shared table, space for materials and the room can be arranged for group activities.

Is ROOM Jūrmala suitable for photoshoots and video filming?

Yes, the venue works for family photoshoots, children’s photoshoots, small video shoots and content creation. The brick wall, play area and cozy interior provide several visual corners.

Is the venue suitable for yoga, fitness and trainer-led classes?

Yes, the venue can be used for yoga, fitness, physiotherapy, children’s movement classes and small training groups. The setup can be adapted to the group size and class format.

Can seminars and training sessions be held there?

Yes, the venue is suitable for small seminars, lectures, parent classes, corporate training and product presentations. It works for situations where you need a calm environment and a practical work area.

Can the venue be used for children’s camps and development classes?

Yes, the venue can be used for children’s camp days, development classes, activity groups and sessions with toddlers or new parents. Longer bookings can be discussed individually.

How many people is the venue suitable for?

Party packages are available for up to 15 guests and for groups over 15 guests. The most comfortable number depends on the event type, table layout and planned activities.

Where is ROOM Jūrmala located and how can I book it?

ROOM Jūrmala is located at Skolas iela 50, Kauguri, Jūrmala. Free parking is available by the building; bookings can be requested through the website, WhatsApp or by phone at +371 27 850 380.
