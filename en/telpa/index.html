Parties

Children’s parties and birthdays in Jūrmala

A cozy event space for children’s birthdays, family celebrations and small private parties. The room works for calm table celebrations as well as more active children’s games and shared activities.

  • Suitable for children’s parties, family celebrations and small get-togethers with friends.
Workshops

For crafts and painting classes

The space is suitable for drawing, painting, crafts, holiday decor making, themed children’s workshops and small group classes. The large table helps organize materials comfortably, while the cozy atmosphere lets children feel free and safe.

  • Suitable for art, crafts, holiday and themed workshops.
  • Can be combined with a children’s party or camp activity.
Photoshoots

For photoshoots and video filming

Suitable for family photoshoots, children’s portraits, celebration photos, small brand content and social media video filming. The room has natural light, a brick wall, a warm interior and enough space to change shots without complicated rearranging.

For photoshoots, it is important that the setting feels clean, calm and not overloaded. This space gives you a cozy background that can be complemented with your own props, balloons, flowers or seasonal decorations.

  • Suitable for family, children’s, celebration and lifestyle photoshoots.
  • A good option for content filming, reels and small brand shoots.
  • The room can be booked by the hour.
Classes

For yoga and fitness classes

The space works for trainers, physiotherapists, children’s movement instructors, yoga or fitness groups. It is compact enough to feel personal, but spacious enough for children or a small adult group.

  • Suitable for yoga, fitness, children’s movement classes and small training groups.
  • Can be used for regular classes or a one-time event.
  • A convenient space for instructors who need a private and cozy environment.
Camps and seminars

Children’s camps, development classes and seminars

The space can be used for children’s camp activities, educational classes, parent and child meetups, small lectures or seminars. It suits formats where a calm environment, shared table, visible group and quick move from conversation to practical activity matter.

This is a good choice for small teams, educators and class leaders who need a place in Jūrmala without the cost of a large hall and without unnecessary formality.

  • Suitable for children’s camp days, development classes and educational activities.
  • Works for small seminars, lectures and practical sessions.
  • Longer bookings can be arranged individually.
